Help & shortcuts

Workflow: pick a template → drop your screenshot on the device → edit the heading → Export. Done in minutes.

Screenshots: drag & drop an image anywhere on the canvas — it fills the selected device frame (or the top-most device). You can also use the Upload button in the Design tab.

Move drag · Constrain hold Shift · Resize corner/edge handles · Rotate handle above selection (Shift snaps 15°)

Multi-select Shift-click layers (canvas or list) · Select all ⌘A — then move, align, distribute, duplicate or delete together.

Nudge ↑↓←→ (+Shift ×10) · Duplicate ⌘D · Delete ⌫ · Reorder layer ⌘]/⌘[

Pro extras: drop several screenshots at once to auto-build one screen per shot · background style "Shot" blurs your screenshot into the backdrop · "Panoramic" flows one background across the whole set · ⊞ toggles safe-area guides (never exported) · devices have a 3D tilt slider.

Two screens, one picture: drag a device (or any layer) past the left/right edge — the part that overflows automatically continues on the neighboring screen at the exact offset, so side-by-side store screenshots read as one wide image. Toggle via "Link screens" in Canvas settings.

Award badges: Add → Award / laurel badge. Pick laurel, laurel + stars, or circle seal; edit the three text lines (or choose a preset), colors and font — great for "Editor's Choice" style credibility strips.
